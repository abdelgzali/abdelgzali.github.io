About

Before I began my journey as a front end developer I was just a 15 year old kid creating avatars and signatures on photoshop, selling them to club penguin/gaiaonline players for in game currency. My fascination with design lead me to learn HTML/CSS as a teen and create custom Myspace and Tumblr themes. Soon after I got my first real client, and my first real paying gig: a simple WordPress design with WooCommerce for e-commerce functionality. Fast forward several years, at this point I've graduated from college, with three years of professional Front End development experience.
